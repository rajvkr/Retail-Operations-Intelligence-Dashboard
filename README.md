# 🛒 Retail Operations Intelligence Dashboard

**Interactive retail analytics dashboard built with Python, Dash, Plotly, and statistical modeling for sales, inventory, profitability, and waste insights.**

---

## 🎯 Project Objective

Transform retail transactional data into an interactive analytics solution that helps monitor business performance, optimize inventory, evaluate pricing strategies, minimize waste, and support data-driven decision-making.

---

## ⚙️ Technology Stack

- **Python**
- **Pandas & NumPy**
- **Dash**
- **Plotly & Plotly Express**
- **Statsmodels (OLS Regression)**
- **Excel**

---

## 🚀 What This Dashboard Delivers

- 📈 Sales trend analysis across monthly and quarterly periods
- 💰 Profitability tracking by category and product
- 📦 Inventory and reorder level monitoring
- ♻️ Waste volume analysis and trend identification
- 🎯 Discount impact on revenue
- 🥗 Dietary preference and product demand insights
- ⏰ Sales quantity analysis by dayparts
- 📊 Interactive visualizations with drill-down exploration

---

## 🧠 Analytical Techniques

- Time-Series Analysis
- Rolling Average Analysis
- OLS Linear Regression
- KPI Aggregation
- Category & Item-Level Performance Analysis
- Inventory & Waste Analytics
- Profit Margin Analysis
- Discount Effectiveness Evaluation

---

## 💡 Business Value

- Identified high-performing products and categories through interactive sales analysis.
- Improved inventory planning using reorder-level insights.
- Evaluated pricing and discount strategies to understand revenue impact.
- Monitored waste trends to support operational efficiency.
- Delivered an interactive dashboard for faster, data-driven business decisions.
